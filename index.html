About Yogacorp


The goal of Yogacorp is to give men and women of all ages convenient access to the overwhelming benefits of yoga… without chanting or teaching spirituality. We believe in developing a practice that is individualized to match each student’s level of ability and skill.

Best of all, you create your own yoga experience by choosing private or group sessions and whether classes are held in your home or workplace.

Our signature yoga practice is designed specifically for men (also a great work out for women). This intense and physically challenging workout is specifically engineered to increase mobility, endurance, and core strength. We focus on developing range of motion and flexibility to train muscle groups to work together for increased strength and stamina. The result is improved performance and stamina in any sport while reducing the risk of injury.

About David


Yogacorp Founder and Instructor

Over 10 years ago, David turned to yoga as means to help enhance his already active life. As his own practice deepened, he soon discovered that his improved core strength, mobility, and endurance was helping with all his other activities like cycling, hiking, golf, and canoeing.

The more David developed his own practice, the more he wanted to bring the positive nature of yoga to men and women of all ages and experience. That’s when he decided to train as a yoga instructor.


Training
David completed a 280-hour yoga Teaching Training with Yogi Jason Lu, Founder and co-owner of Yoga Tree. David has taught regular yoga classes at LA Fitness.

David's yoga philosophy
David firmly believes that finding inner wellbeing through yoga is a wonderful individual experience students need to attain on their own. That’s why David does not preach or teach the spiritual side of yoga.
